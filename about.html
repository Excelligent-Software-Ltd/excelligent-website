Building the Future of Educational Technology

We're on a mission to solve education's retention problem with intelligent, research-backed learning solutions.

Our Mission

At Excelligent Software, we believe that true learning isn't measured by test scores—it's measured by what students remember and can apply weeks, months, and years later.

We're building a new generation of educational technology designed around one core principle: retention is everything.

Traditional educational software focuses on engagement metrics, completion rates, and short-term performance. These are important, but they're not enough. If students forget what they've learned shortly after the test, we haven't truly educated them—we've just trained them to perform.

Our approach is different. Every product we build is designed from the ground up to maximise long-term retention using proven cognitive science principles. We're not just making learning more convenient—we're making it more effective.

Why We Started with Spelling

Spelling is the perfect starting point for our retention-focused approach. It's a foundational skill that every student needs to master, yet it's plagued by the same retention problems we see across education:

  • Students cram for weekly tests then forget the words
  • Teachers spend hours administering and marking tests
  • One-size-fits-all lists don't meet individual needs
  • There's no systematic approach to ensuring long-term retention

With Cadmus Spelling, we've proven that intelligent, personalised revision can transform outcomes. Students truly master and retain their spellings—not just for Friday's test, but for life.

But we're not stopping at spelling.
